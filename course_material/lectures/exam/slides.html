❖ Environment


What you need, to do the exam

  • a web browser
  • PostgreSQL (v12), Python 3.7, Psycopg2
  • enough space to create a small-ish database
Where might you find such an environment?
  • on Grieg
  • on the VLab servers
  • on your home machine
